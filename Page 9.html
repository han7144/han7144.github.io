Do arctic foxes change color?
Yes, arctic foxes change the color of their fur depending on the season which makes it harder for predators to spot them. The arctic fox coat color will be white during winter months to blend in with the snow, then they will switch to earthy tones such as brown or grey during summer months.
Arctic foxes are best known for their thick white fur that helps them blend in with the snow of the arctic and ice floes. This provides camouflage to protect them from their predators.
